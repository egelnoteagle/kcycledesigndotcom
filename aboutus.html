"Better and Better"

Introduction

Mike Furtek owned and operated M&D Custom, Corp. for 31 years in South Holland, Illinois. He has been providing one-of-a kind services in customized painting, welding/fabricating of parts for motorcycles, and building/designing of motorcycles for customers to show or for personal enjoyment.

He has forty one years of experience in the world of customizing and has operated three successful customizing shops: The Abbey, M&G Collision, and M&D Custom. Although primarily working with motorcycles, Mike has also customized Corvettes at "The Abbey" in Riverdale, Illinois as well as the National Champion Show Car Award and numerous Professional Race car teams in NHRA. He has created custom paint and designs to Harley Davidson Motorcycle Company for use in their custom paint program and custom paint work for Speed Limit Motorcycles in the sport bike industry, numbering in the thousands.

Throughout his career Mike has aquired several awards and publications have shown his much of his work as well as receiving acknowledgement from national peers.

The Product

Mike Furtek is now directing his talent toward customizing high-end road/triathlon bikes for the bike enthusiast whom desire to take their bike to the next level. Mike Furtek, along with Kathy Petri, has formed Kcycle, a customized paint shop on the cutting edge for those wishing to take their bikes to a higher level that truly makes a one-of-a kind customized bike. Mike offers all possibilities with his vision, insight, and specialization in mixing of paint colors, designs, and ideas. Mike not only specializes in customized bike frames, but offers customized bike helmets, disc wheels, and project team bikes. Kcycle does not provide just something pretty. There are no other businesses in the surrounding area that offer what Kcycle provides with respect to uniqueness.

Pricing Strategy

Kcycle has a set standard for work provided. Due to the uniqueness of customers, the overall costs vary. The company provides additional discounts for team bikes and bulk bike shop orders. Due to offering a number of services in the same facility, just tell Mike what you want, he can help you obtain your stand-out uniqueness.
